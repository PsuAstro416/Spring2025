<div class="markdown"><h3>How AI is advancing AST</h3><h4>Cosmology:</h4><ul><li><p>Deep learning for parameter inferences on large scale structure &amp; Comsic Microwave Background</p></li><li><p>Neural network surrogate models for expensive N-body simulations</p></li><li><p>Specialized architectures (e.g., Graph Neural Networks) to work with structure of cosmological simulation data sets</p></li></ul><h4>Time-domain Astronomy:</h4><ul><li><p>Neural networks for real-time classification &amp; early detection of transit events (e.g., supernovae, graviational waves) </p></li><li><p>Irregular sampling and heterogeneous noise characteristics of astronomical time-series data (?)</p></li><li><p>Unsupervised techniques (e.g., clustering algorithsm) to identify new classes of transients</p></li></ul><h4>Exoplanet Research:</h4><ul><li><p>Machine learning to distinguish planetary signals from stellar variability</p></li><li><p>Efficient exploration of highly multimodal posterior distributions (e.g., microlensing events)</p></li></ul><h4>Stellar Astrophysics:</h4><ul><li><p>Spectral analysis of surveys (SDSS) to extract physical parameters from low-resolution and/or low signal-to-noise spectra</p></li><li><p>Automating pipelines for analyzing eclipsing binary stars to search for stellar-mass black holes</p></li><li><p>Neural networks to analyze stellar oscilation data to characterize stellar interiors and evolution</p></li></ul><h4>Galactic Evolution:</h4><ul><li><p>Deep learning for extracting galaxy morphology from surveys</p></li><li><p>Generating synthetic datasets for augmenting training data</p></li><li><p>Reducing cost of inference with complex models</p></li></ul><h3>Heliophysics:</h3><ul><li><p>Ensemble methods for Forecasting space weather</p></li><li><p>Identifying and classifying sunspot groups and photospheric vector magnetic fields</p></li><li><p>Neural fields for reconstruction of solar surface features to provide more complete picture of dynamics</p></li></ul><h3>Gravitational Waves:</h3><ul><li><p>Deep learning for signal processing to separate true GW signals from noise</p></li><li><p>Rapid identification and localization to support follow-up observations</p></li><li><p>Reduce computational cost compared to matched filtering</p></li></ul></div>
